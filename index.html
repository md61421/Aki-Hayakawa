Aki Hayakawa

Gender: Male

Birthplace: Hokkaido, Japan

Contracted devils: Fox Devil

Occupation: Public safety Devil Hunter

Aki Hayakawa (早川 アキ Hayakawa Aki?) was a Public Safety Devil Hunter, working under Makima's special squad.[1] He had contracts with the Curse Devil and Future Devil, and a former contract with the Fox Devil. He was killed and possessed by 20% of the Gun Devil, thus becoming the host of the Gun Fiend (銃の魔人 Jū no Majin?).

He was a deuteragonist of the Introduction arc through International Assassins arc of the Public Safety Saga, and an unwitting main antagonist of the Gun Devil arc as the Gun Fiend.

Human Form

Aki Hayakawa was a handsome young man with medium-length black hair and blue eyes. He usually wore his hair in a topknot style. He often wore the Public Safety Devil Hunter suit and tie. He also donned earrings and usually carried his sword on his back.
